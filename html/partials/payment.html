Application Payment

Your application will be sent to FAST Demonstration School upon completion of this payment portion. Please make sure that you have correctly entered all the required information. Once you have completed this payment section, changes to your financial information may only be made by contacting your school.

In order to complete this payment section, you must have a Visa, a MasterCard, an American Express or a voucher number from your school. If you do not have any of these, please contact your school for alternate forms of payment.
